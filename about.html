THE STORY OF FERRERO

Embark on a journey through time and discover the rich history of Ferrero, a story of passion, innovation, and the pursuit of excellence.

A Visionary Beginning: It all started in 1946 when Pietro Ferrero, a visionary confectioner from Italy, set out to create something extraordinary. With a humble beginning in the town of Alba, he dreamed of crafting confections that would bring joy to people's lives.

The Birth of Nutella: In 1964, Pietro's son, Michele Ferrero, introduced the world to Nutella, a delicious hazelnut spread that would revolutionize breakfast tables everywhere. Combining hazelnuts, cocoa, and a touch of magic, Nutella quickly became an icon of taste and happiness.

Innovating with Rocher: Building on the success of Nutella, Ferrero continued to innovate. In 1982, Ferrero Rocher was introduced, offering a luxurious combination of creamy chocolate, crispy wafer, and roasted hazelnuts. It soon became synonymous with elegance and indulgence.

Expanding Globally: With a commitment to quality and a passion for delighting consumers, Ferrero expanded its presence globally. From Europe to Asia, North America to Australia, Ferrero products are cherished by millions of people in over 170 countries worldwide.

A Legacy of Excellence: Today, Ferrero remains true to its heritage while embracing the future with innovation and sustainability at its core. From Kinder to Tic Tac, Ferrero continues to create iconic brands that bring joy to every moment.

Empowering Communities: Beyond crafting exceptional confections, Ferrero is dedicated to making a positive impact on society. Through initiatives like the Ferrero Farming Values program and the Kinder+Sport project, we're empowering communities and fostering a brighter future for generations to come.

Join Our Journey: As we celebrate our past and look to the future, we invite you to join us on our journey of sweetness and joy. From our family to yours, thank you for being a part of the Ferrero story.
