OUR FIRST PRODUCT ON THE MARKET

The first product launched by Ferrero on the market was the iconic "Giandujot," introduced in 1946.
Giandujot was a chocolate-hazelnut confection created by Pietro Ferrero in his pastry shop in Alba, Italy.
It was crafted using a combination of cocoa and locally sourced hazelnuts, which were abundant in the Piedmont region where the company originated.
Giandujot quickly gained popularity for its rich flavor and creamy texture, laying the foundation for Ferrero's future success in the confectionery industry.
